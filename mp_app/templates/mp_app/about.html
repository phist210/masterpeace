Masterpeace

Masterpeace is a social network for artists. The site mimics the art gallery experience by using a side scroll display, and fosters a collaborative and supportive environment. We limit user feedback to only positive reactions, and allow artists control over how users react to their individual ‘peaces’. Users also can send messages to each other and flag inappropriate content.
We ask everyone simply do one thing: treat others like you want to be treated.
